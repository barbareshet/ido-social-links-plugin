Easily add social networks links to your WP site

== Description ==
This plugin helps you adding your social networks links both to each post or globally on your website using a shortcode

There are 4 social networks currently availiable, Facebook, Google+, Twitter And Instagram


== Installation ==
1. Download the zip file.
2. Upload and activate.
3. Go to  the settings page and add your social networks links.
4. You can choose a color for your links, to adjust your site design.
5. In order to show the social links on Posts / Feed, you must check the 'enable' options.
6. You can add the social link via shortcode too, in any xontent area (widgets / post content area)
